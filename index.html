How to Open the Console on Visual Studio

To Open the Console, first you have to Go live on the page you type the code on(Make sure the page it opens on is Chrome). Next you have to right click on the Page and when you right click there should be an inspect,click on it. Then on the Right side there will be a big kind of like tab there. On the top of that there will be a console option,click that. Now,Your console is open!
